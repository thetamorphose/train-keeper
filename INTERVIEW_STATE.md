да2# INTERVIEW_STATE: Train Keeper

## Block 0: Business Context
- **Essence & Scale:** Mobile-first web app for tracking diverse workout progress (gym, yoga, cycling).
- **Primary Target Action:** Recording varied workouts and exercises (sets, values) with minimal friction.
- **Success Metrics:** User efficiency (speed of logging) and personal satisfaction with usability.
- **Platform:** Mobile First (Android focus).

## Block 1: Polar Profiles
1. Utilitarian ↔ Expressive: 3
2. Spatial ↔ Informative: 3
3. Native ↔ Immersive: 1
4. Sharp Geometry ↔ Soft Organic: 5
5. Typography-led ↔ Media-rich: 1
6. Achromatic ↔ Vibrant: 5

## Block 2: Brand Archetypes
- **Explorer (Искатель):** Focus on diverse workouts and self-discovery through movement.
- **Caregiver (Заботливый):** Soft, psychological motivation and support ("You can do it!").

## Block 2.1: Brand Persona
- **First Impression:** Joy and relief: "Wow, tracking progress can be this simple and easy." Overcoming the barrier of complexity.
- **Association:** A mountain stream ("Water carves the stone"). Diverse movements (currents) merging into a single powerful flow of health and happiness.
- **Final Aftertaste:** "Now I know how to build a habit of tracking my progress."

## Block 3: Market & Taboos
- **Benchmarks:** Soundcloud (immesive/simple), Eagle.cool (UX for speed & complex data).
- **Taboos:** NO Futurism/Tech-focus, NO "Muscle/Athlete" imagery, NO forced criteria.
- **Vibe:** Natural, personal, habit-forming, fluid.
